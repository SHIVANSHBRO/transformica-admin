Delete Your Account

Operated by Novablitz Smart Fitness Pvt. Ltd. · Privacy Policy · Terms of Service

This page explains how to delete your Transformica account (package com.transformica.app) and what happens to your data when you do. You can delete your account yourself from inside the app, or ask us to do it for you.

Deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted it cannot be restored, and neither you nor our support team can recover your logs, photos or messages. If you only want to stop being billed, cancel your subscription instead — see below.

Option 1 — Delete it yourself in the app

  1. Open the Transformica app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Privacy & data sharing.
  3. Tap Delete my account.
  4. Confirm when prompted. Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2 — Ask us to delete it

If you can no longer sign in or have already uninstalled the app, email us from the address registered on your account:

To: info@transformica.in
Subject: Delete my account
Include: the email address and mobile number registered on your Transformica account

We verify that the request comes from the account holder before deleting anything, then complete the deletion within 30 days and confirm by email. We may contact you if we need more information to confirm your identity.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your profile and account credentials
  • All logged data — meals, workouts, weight and vitals, water, steps and sleep
  • Progress photos, meal photos and any other uploads
  • Messages and voice notes exchanged with your coach
  • Your conversations with Aasha, our AI assistant
  • Diet and workout plans created for you
  • Body scan and form-check records

What we keep, and for how long

Nothing retained above is used to contact you or to rebuild your profile.

Cancelling your subscription

Deleting your account does not by itself cancel an active UPI AutoPay or card mandate. If you have a paid subscription, cancel the mandate in your UPI or banking app (or email us and we will cancel it from the Razorpay side) so no further payments are taken. Fixed-term plans already paid for are not refunded on deletion — see the Terms of Service.

Questions

Contact our Grievance Officer at info@transformica.in. Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 you may also request a copy of your data, or correction of it, rather than deletion.
