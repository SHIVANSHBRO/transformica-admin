Privacy Policy

Effective 13 July 2026 · Operated by Novablitz Smart Fitness Pvt. Ltd. · Terms of Service

Transformica is a fitness coaching service made up of the Transformica member app, the Transformica Coach app, and related services (together, the “Service”), operated by Novablitz Smart Fitness Private Limited (“Novablitz”, “we”, “us”), a company incorporated in India. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, who we share it with, and the rights and controls you have. By using the Service you consent to this policy.

The short version:

  • We collect the fitness and health data you log so your coach and our AI tools can actually coach you. We never sell your data and we show no third-party ads.
  • Camera-based form checks and body scans are processed entirely on your device — the video never leaves your phone.
  • Conversations with your coach can be reviewed by authorised Novablitz staff for safety and quality.
  • Some features send specific data (like a meal photo) to our AI provider to generate a result; our provider is contractually barred from training on it.
  • You can delete your account and all your data yourself, in the app, at any time.

1. Data we collect

Account & profile

  • Name, email address, and phone number (optional); your password is stored only as a secure hash by our authentication provider.
  • Profile details you provide: date of birth, sex, height, starting and target weight, fitness goal, dietary preference, activity level, and daily targets (calories, protein, steps, water).
  • Your subscription plan and its expiry date.

Health & fitness data you log

  • Body weight and other vitals, body measurements, workout logs, meals and nutrition, water intake, and daily steps.
  • Menstrual cycle data (period dates and cycle length), if you choose to use cycle tracking.
  • Symptom answers and results from the nutritional-deficiency checker, lab-marker values you enter or files you upload, and health intake forms you complete for your coach.
  • Steps and activity data from Health Connect (Android) or Apple Health, only with your explicit permission — see section 6.

Photos, voice & camera

  • Progress photos, meal photos, and challenge submission photos you choose to upload. These are stored in private storage and are visible only to you, your assigned coach, and authorised Novablitz staff.
  • Voice notes you exchange with your coach in chat.
  • Form checks and body scans use your camera, but the video is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded or recorded. We store only derived, non-visual data: joint-position coordinates, rep counts, and technique scores.

Messages

  • Your chat with your coach (text and voice notes).
  • Your conversations with Aasha, our AI assistant.

Payments

  • Payments are processed by Razorpay. We never receive or store your card, UPI, or bank details. We keep a record of the plan purchased, the amount, and the payment status.

Device & technical

  • Push-notification tokens, app version, and device type.
  • Crash reports and product-usage analytics (which screens and features are used), to keep the app stable and improve it. These are pseudonymous and never include your health logs, photos, or messages.

2. How we use your data

  • To coach you: your assigned coach sees your logs, progress, photos you share, and messages so they can build plans and hold you accountable.
  • To personalise the app: calorie/protein/step targets, workout plans, cycle-aware insights, streaks, challenges, and the weekly Sunday Report.
  • To run AI features you invoke (section 3).
  • To keep you safe: safety limits in AI features and human review described in section 4.
  • To operate the Service: account management, payments, customer support, and service notifications (push and in-app), such as coach replies, class start alerts, and streak reminders. You can turn off notification categories in your device settings.
  • To improve the Service: aggregated, pseudonymous usage analytics and crash diagnostics.

We do not sell your personal data, show third-party advertising, or use your health data for advertising of any kind.

3. AI features

Some features are powered by Anthropic's Claude models. When you use one of these features, only the data needed for that feature is sent to Anthropic's API for processing:

  • Food photo scan: the meal photo you submit, to estimate its contents and calories.
  • Body-composition estimate: the photos you submit for that estimate.
  • Aasha assistant: your conversation text and, where you have given in-app consent, relevant fitness context (like recent logs) to personalise answers.
  • Form-check review: a numeric summary of your workout (angles, reps, scores) — never camera footage.

Under our agreement with Anthropic, your data is not used to train their models. AI outputs are estimates and general fitness information, not medical advice — see the Terms of Service. AI features have daily usage limits per account.

4. Human review of coach conversations

Chats between members and coaches — including voice notes — are not private between you and your coach. Authorised Novablitz staff can review these conversations for member safety, coaching quality, dispute resolution, and to enforce our terms. Please don't share sensitive information unrelated to your coaching (such as financial details) in chat.

6. Health Connect & device health data

With your explicit permission, the app reads activity data (such as steps) from Health Connect on Android or Apple Health on iOS. This data is used only to display and track your fitness progress inside Transformica and to inform your coach's guidance. It is never used for advertising, never sold, and never shared with third parties except the storage provider that hosts all your Transformica data (Supabase). Our use of Health Connect data complies with Google's Health Connect permissions policy, including its Limited Use requirements. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device's Health Connect or Health settings, and the app will continue to work without it.

7. Storage & security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and stored with our hosting provider, Supabase.
  • Access is enforced at the database row level: you can only read your own records, coaches can only read their assigned clients, and staff access is limited to authorised roles. Staff actions in the admin panel are logged to an audit trail.
  • Photos and voice notes live in private storage buckets and are served only through short-lived signed links.
  • No system is perfectly secure; if a breach affecting your personal data occurs, we will notify you and the Data Protection Board of India as required by law.

8. Retention & deleting your account

  • Your data is retained while your account is active so the Service can function.
  • You can delete your account in the app: Profile → Privacy & data sharing → Delete my account. This permanently deletes your profile, logs, photos, voice notes, messages, and AI conversations. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.
  • We retain payment/transaction records for the period required by Indian tax and accounting law, even after account deletion.
  • Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged on the backup rotation cycle (up to 30 days).

9. Your rights (Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023)

As a Data Principal under India's DPDP Act, you have the right to:

  • Access a summary of the personal data we hold about you and how it is processed;
  • Correct or update inaccurate or incomplete data (most profile data is editable in the app);
  • Erase your data (via in-app account deletion or by writing to us);
  • Withdraw consent at any time, with effect for future processing;
  • Grievance redressal through our Grievance Officer (section 12), and thereafter the Data Protection Board of India;
  • Nominate another individual to exercise these rights in the event of death or incapacity.

To exercise any right, email info@transformica.in from your registered email address.

10. Age requirement

Transformica is for adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18; if we learn we have, we will delete it. If you believe a minor is using the Service, please contact us.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Service evolves. Material changes will be announced in the app before they take effect, and the "Effective" date above will be updated. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
